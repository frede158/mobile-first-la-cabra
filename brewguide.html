BREW GUIDE

FRENCH PRESS

20g coffee

300g Water

0:00 Pour all water

4:00 Break the coffee crust and stir 6 times

8:00 Put in plunger and pour into cup

ESPRESSO

18g coffee

50g Espresso in the cup

Brewtime: 18-22 seconds

COLD BREW

20g coffee

300g Water

Mix water and coffee

Wait for 24 hours and drain

Then enjoy!
